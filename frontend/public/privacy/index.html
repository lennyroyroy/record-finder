Privacy & Terms

Last updated: February 2026

Privacy Policy

The short version: your data lives in your browser. We don't collect it, store it, or sell it. There's no account on our end — just you and your local storage.

Your data stays in your browser

Everything you add to Spin or Stream — your wantlist, scan results, collection, and preferences — is stored in your browser's localStorage. None of it is sent to our servers. If you clear your browser data, it's gone. We have no copy.

Discogs OAuth

When you log in with Discogs, your browser receives a signed token directly from Discogs. We use that token to fetch your wantlist and collection on your behalf. We never store your Discogs credentials or your token on our servers.

External APIs we call

Our backend acts as a proxy to two external services:

Discogs API — to search the marketplace and fetch your wantlist/collection. Discogs' own privacy policy applies to data they hold about you.

open.er-api.com — for currency conversion rates. We send only the currency codes needed (e.g. "EUR", "GBP"), not any personal data.

What we don't do

No analytics. No tracking pixels. No ad networks. No cookies (beyond what your browser may set for session handling). No data sold to third parties. Ever.

Waitlist email

If you submitted your email on the landing page, it's held by Mailchimp and used only to notify you when the app launches. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email we send.

Children's privacy

Spin or Stream is not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect any data from minors.

Spin or Stream is provided as-is, without warranty of any kind. Marketplace prices, shipping estimates, and currency conversions are approximate and may be outdated. Always verify final prices on the source platform before purchasing.

Not affiliated with Discogs

This is an independent project. We use the Discogs API under their terms but we are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to Discogs, Inc.

The app may change

We may update, modify, or discontinue features at any time. Since your data lives in your browser, you're responsible for any exports or backups you need.

Acceptable use

Don't use Spin or Stream in ways that abuse the Discogs API or violate Discogs' terms of service. The scan delay in the app exists to respect their rate limits — don't try to circumvent it.
