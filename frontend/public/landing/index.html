You already know what you want. That's the easy part.

The hard part is everything that comes next. The Bandcamp vinyl you love ships from the UK for $30. The only US copy on Discogs is a first pressing at $100. There's probably a perfectly good reissue somewhere for $20, but finding it means five tabs, two searches, and a gut feeling.

That's the part that gets expensive. Not because you made a bad decision — because comparing everything at once is genuinely hard.

What it does

Spin or Stream does the
comparing for you.

Connect your Discogs wantlist and Spin or Stream pulls together what actually matters: the cheapest US listing, international options with real shipping estimates, Bandcamp pricing for the vinyl and the digital, and links to find reissues when they exist.

Buy the cheap vinyl. Buy the expensive original. Buy digital and wait for a better deal. Or skip it entirely. That's your call — Spin or Stream just makes sure you're making it with the full picture.

The pipeline

Three stages. One clean decision.

Sync your Discogs wantlist, run a comparison across every option, and move it to your collection when you pull the trigger. A clear record of what you own and how you got it.

01
Wantlist
Sync your Discogs wantlist or add records manually by pasting a Bandcamp, Pitchfork, or Discogs URL. Scan marketplace prices across your whole list.
02
Compare
Every option side by side. US and international listings with shipping estimates, Bandcamp vinyl and digital, and links to find it elsewhere.
03
Collection
Syncs with your Discogs collection. See what you already own alongside what you're hunting — so you're never comparing something you already have.
